Given an array of integers,&nbsp;<font color="#af5f00">return</font>&nbsp;indices of the two numbers such that they add up to a specific target.<br>
<br>
You may assume that each input would have exactly one solution.<br>
<br>
<font color="#af5f00">Example</font>:<br>
Given nums = [<font color="#c00000">2</font>,&nbsp;<font color="#c00000">7</font>,&nbsp;<font color="#c00000">11</font>,&nbsp;<font color="#c00000">15</font>], target =&nbsp;<font color="#c00000">9</font>,<br>
<br>
Because nums[<font color="#c00000">0</font>] + nums[<font color="#c00000">1</font>] =&nbsp;<font color="#c00000">2</font>&nbsp;+&nbsp;<font color="#c00000">7</font>&nbsp;=&nbsp;<font color="#c00000">9</font>,<br>
<font color="#af5f00">return</font>&nbsp;[<font color="#c00000">0</font>,&nbsp;<font color="#c00000">1</font>].<br>
<br>
UPDATE (<font color="#c00000">2016</font>/<font color="#c00000">2</font>/<font color="#c00000">13</font>):<br>
The&nbsp;<font color="#af5f00">return</font>&nbsp;format had been changed to zero-based indices. Please read the above updated description carefully.<br>
<br>
<br>
<font color="#af5f00">public</font>&nbsp;<font color="#008000">int</font>[] twoSum(<font color="#008000">int</font>[] numbers,&nbsp;<font color="#008000">int</font>&nbsp;target) {<br>
&nbsp;&nbsp; Map&lt;Integer, Integer&gt; map =&nbsp;<font color="#af5f00">new</font>&nbsp;HashMap&lt;&gt;();<br>
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;<font color="#af5f00">for</font>&nbsp;(<font color="#008000">int</font>&nbsp;i =&nbsp;<font color="#c00000">0</font>; i &lt; numbers.length; i++) {<br>
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;<font color="#008000">int</font>&nbsp;x = numbers[i];<br>
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;<font color="#af5f00">if</font>&nbsp;(map.containsKey(target - x)) {<br>
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;<font color="#af5f00">return</font>&nbsp;<font color="#af5f00">new</font>&nbsp;<font color="#008000">int</font>[] { map.get(target - x), i};<br>
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;}<br>
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;map.put(x, i);<br>
&nbsp;&nbsp; }<br>
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;<font color="#af5f00">throw</font>&nbsp;<font color="#af5f00">new</font>&nbsp;IllegalArgumentException(<font color="#c00000">&quot;No two sum solution&quot;</font>);<br>
}<br>
